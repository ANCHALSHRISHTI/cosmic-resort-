Our Menu

A delightful journey through the diverse cuisines of India, bringing you authentic flavors from North, South, East, and West!

🥶 North Indian Cuisine

  • Punjab: Butter Chicken – Creamy tomato-based chicken curry.
  • Kashmir: Rogan Josh – Slow-cooked aromatic lamb curry.
  • Uttar Pradesh: Tunday Kabab – Melt-in-mouth mutton kebabs.

🌴 South Indian Cuisine

  • Tamil Nadu: Dosa with Sambar – Crispy fermented crepe with lentil soup.
  • Kerala: Kerala Fish Curry – Tangy coconut-based fish curry.
  • Andhra Pradesh: Hyderabadi Biryani – Fragrant dum-cooked rice.

🐅 Eastern Indian Cuisine

  • West Bengal: Shorshe Ilish – Hilsa fish in mustard gravy.
  • Odisha: Dalma – Lentils with vegetables and raw papaya.
  • Assam: Assamese Thali – Rice, fish, green leafy vegetables, and dal.

🌄 Western Indian Cuisine

  • Rajasthan: Dal Baati Churma – Wheat dumplings with lentils.
  • Gujarat: Dhokla – Steamed gram flour cake.
  • Goa: Pork Vindaloo – Fiery, vinegar-based pork curry.
